College Preparation Guide

Documents to Prepare

  • Marksheet (10th & 12th)
  • Transfer Certificate
  • Passport Size Photos
  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.)

Admission Process Steps

  1. Research colleges and courses
  2. Fill out application forms
  3. Appear for entrance exams (if required)
  4. Attend counseling/interview sessions
  5. Submit required documents and fees

Common Entrance Exams

JEE
Joint Entrance Examination for engineering courses
NEET
National Eligibility cum Entrance Test for medical courses
CUET
Common University Entrance Test for various UG programs
